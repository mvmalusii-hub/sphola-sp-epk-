Story

Sphola SP (Sipho Thanta) is a hip-hop artist from Engcobo, now based in Cape Town. He blends raw storytelling with melodic flows across hip-hop, Afro pop, and amapiano.

Currently working on the EP "Uthandolwethu", Sphola SP recently dropped the single "I Want It All" (feat. Blaq Chain & Vavi RSA). The music video for "Bhatala" is coming soon.
