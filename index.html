Story

Sphola SP is the stage name of Sipho Thanta, a hip-hop artist from Engcobo now making waves in Cape Town. Known for his versatile sound, Sphola moves seamlessly between hip-hop, Afro pop, and amapiano, delivering music that’s raw, melodic, and bold.

Drawing inspiration from South African heavyweights like Kideo from Backyard Crew, Kwesta, and Emtee, Sphola started his journey with a focus on authentic storytelling and hard-hitting ambition. His music reflects his roots and his grind, connecting with listeners through real experiences and genre-blending production.

Sphola SP is currently working on his upcoming EP titled "Uthandolwethu" while preparing for the release of the music video for his single "Bhatala". His catalog, including previous release "I Want It All" feat. Blaq Chain & Vavi RSA, is available on all major digital platforms.
